About Us

We are Badger Beard Games, a small but humble group of talented creators based in the UK, trying to make a positive impact on the gaming industry. We started off as a group of friends with a like-minded passion for gaming and creating games, and have since grown to a team of 6 individuals, dedicating themselves to our first title, Stickys.

The team has well over 40 years of collective professional experience in the gaming industry. We have experienced the highs and lows, both from the perspective of those that create games, and from the perspective of those that play games. We believe that with our passion, knowledge and experience, we can help to shape the gaming industry in a highly positive way by focusing on giving players something valuable to spend their time on, rather than simply treating players as a statistic. Our goal is to create memorable and positively encouraging experiences for all to enjoy.

Stickys

Explore a vibrant world of endless opportunities with your very own customisable Sticky. Solve physics challenges with your Sticky friends as up to 4 of you climb, throw, bonk and wobble your way to the top in order to complete the level. Will you make it with your friendships still intact?

Stickys is inspired by the wobbly chaos of Human: Fall Flat, and the slapstick brawls of Gang Beasts. We have combined the charm of these two games into a modernised physics sandbox that allows for an impressively accurate multiplayer physics simulation. This has resulted in the ability to deliver a game world that is both fun and intuitive, while allowing us to build incredible worlds for you to play within, and hopefully, eventually, create yourselves.

At the heart of it all is our Sticky character. Not only are they entertaining to watch, but the intuitive hand controls allow them to navigate almost any obstacle, and interact with any physical object in a fun and intuitive way. Why not pick up that axe and whack your Sticky friend over the head with it; and then pick up that friend and throw them over the edge? There is not much that isn’t possible with what we have built already.
